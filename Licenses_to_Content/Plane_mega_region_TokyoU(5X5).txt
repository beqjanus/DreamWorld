Plane and Train System OAR in Mega Region from http://www.nsl.tuis.ac.jp/xoops/modules/d3downloads/index.php?page=singlefile&cid=8&lid=23

From Tokyo University of Information Sciences. It's a 5 region mega, with trains and a aeroplane flying game where you fly through hoops in the sky. 

Please attention that Train and Station in OAR are not object that we made. Those are from http://matangdilis.blogspot.com/
But scripts of Train and Rail was made by us.

Mega Region size is 5x5
Please load this file at Root Region.
